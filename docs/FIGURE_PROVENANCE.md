## Notes

1. **Archived scripts**: Many generating scripts are in `scripts/archive/` but the figures they produced are still current and valid.

2. **Floquet output path**: `generate_floquet_plots.py` outputs to `plots/` not `fig/floquet/`. Files may have been manually moved.

3. **On-the-fly computation**: Several scripts compute data on-the-fly rather than loading from pickle files. These take longer to regenerate.

4. **v7 suffix convention**: Publication figures use `_v7_exp` (exponential form) and `_v7_pow2` (power-of-2 form) suffixes for dual-version outputs.
